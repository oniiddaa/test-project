About KTM

Crafting the ultimate riding experience since 1985

Our Legacy

For nearly four decades, KTM has been at the forefront of motorcycle innovation. What started as a small garage operation has evolved into one of the most respected names in the industry.

Our Mission

To deliver unparalleled performance, safety, and style in every motorcycle we build. We believe that riding is more than transportation—it's a way of life.

Our Vision

To lead the motorcycle industry into a sustainable future while preserving the raw thrill and freedom that defines the riding experience.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, and passion drive everything we do. We stand behind every machine that bears our name with unwavering commitment to quality.

38+ Years of Excellence
500K+ Motorcycles Sold
45 Countries Worldwide
200+ Awards Won

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by motorcycle enthusiast James Thunder, our company began with a simple dream: to create motorcycles that embody the spirit of freedom and adventure.

From our first custom-built cruiser to our latest high-performance sport bikes, every KTM is designed with passion and precision. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility combines traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology.

Today, we're proud to serve riders in over 45 countries, united by their love for the open road and the Thunder Motorcycles experience.
